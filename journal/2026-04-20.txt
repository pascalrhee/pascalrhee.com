Day 1 — Claimed pascalrhee.com and got a placeholder live
Bought pascalrhee.com through Cloudflare Registrar. Tried to deploy a simple HTML file via "Create application" and accidentally created a Worker instead of a Pages project uploaded the HTML via drag-and-drop. Hit a DNS verification screen that looked like it needed manual CNAME records but 
just clicking "Check DNS records" worked because Cloudflare auto-created them in the background.Site is live with SSL. What surprised me: DNS and SSL felt intimidating from the outside and turned out to be mostly clicking through screens.  The Workers vs Pages confusion cost me more time than the actual setup. What I don't fully understand yet: why Cloudflare is pushing Workers over Pages, and what the actual difference is for a static site like this.
